About Us

Who we are

Myadmissionlink Educational Consult (MEC) is a Registered, Government Approved and International Accredited Educational Consult. We are into Educational Training and Educational Consultancy. Our Educational Training Programmes include University Foundation Programme(UFP) and Professional Programme(PP). Also the Educational Consultancy includes study abroad processing to our partner universities and School Management System. MEC was established in 2010 and become fully incorporated under the Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in 2013. It was established to fill the gap for the need of qualitative but yet affordable education home and abroad.


Our aim is to create enabling platform that helps in discovering of latent skills, career development, promotion of academic excellence, admission to higher institutions both home and abroad and lots more.

Our Team

As part of our commitment to deliver first class services & training to our clients, we offer a set of professional services for optimum client satisfaction. At the heart of all our services is our dedication to the success of our partners and students. We operate with a team of formal and committed Senior Education Advisers /counselors who understand the need of the students and their parents / sponsors. We firmly believe in best customer service to the students and our client institutions. Our strongly student centered approach to counselling means; we give students the fullest possible information on the available options to help them make sensible decisions.

Our Values

We believe no child is born brilliant, hence there should be equal opportunity to learn and relearn.
